I have set up a profile README that includes my name, branch, year, and a brief note on what I hope to learn and build over the course of my degree.

**LinkedIn** is the professional networking platform of choice for employers, recruiters, and the academic community globally. It is where internship opportunities are listed, where alumni networks are built, and where professional credibility is established over time. I have filled in my Education section with my current degree, institution (VIT Bhopal University), and expected graduation year, keeping the profile clean and minimal at this stage.

**Kaggle** is a data science and machine learning platform that hosts public datasets, coding notebooks, competitions, and structured learning paths. For an aerospace engineering student interested in simulation, analysis, and R&D, Kaggle offers access to datasets related to flight performance, materials, satellite systems, and more — making it a useful complement to my core engineering coursework.

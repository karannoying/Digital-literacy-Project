## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic (Module 1)
Created a one-page visual infographic using **Canva** covering: what digital literacy is, professional online presence, safe internet practices, and useful digital tools for students. Designed with an engineering-student audience in mind.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio (Module 2)
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**. GitHub profile README includes my branch, year, and learning goals related to aerospace simulation and avionics software tools.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms (Module 3)
Completed a beginner challenge on **CodeChef** and built a 5-question **Google Form** — *Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz* — for batchmates. The quiz covers safe internet practices, professional communication, and cybersecurity basics.

📋 **Google Form Quiz Link:** [[Link](https://forms.gle/M7LfQu75J5QDyY68A)]

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette (Module 4)
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting a deadline extension from a professor, and one expressing interest in an internship. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist for college students.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness (Module 5)
Researched **UPI / Online Payment Fraud** — how it happens, who it targets, and its consequences. Produced an 8-point prevention checklist with specific UPI safety tips and reporting channels including the **National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)** and **Helpline 1930**.
